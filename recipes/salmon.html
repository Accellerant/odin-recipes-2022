Blackened Salmon Recipe

A bold and Insanely Fast Entree!

Salmon has always been a nutritious option for all,
but why go for something plain when you can give yourself
insaneo flavor-town?

The Blackened Salmon recipe will have you learn how easy
it is to make this 15 minute dish with complementing flavor
and texture by having a crispy outside and a melt in your mouth inside!

Ingredients

  • 3 Tablespoons Canola or Light Olive Oil.
  • 1 Teaspoon Fine Sea Salt or Kosher Salt.
  • 2-3 Tablespoons of BLackened Seasoning.
  • 4 6 oz. Salmon Fillets with Skin, Bones Removed.
  • 4 Sprigs Fresh Thyme
  • 1 Teaspoon Fresh Oregano Leaves
  • 1 Lemon (zest & juice)

Steps

  1. Heat oil to smoking in a skillet, medium heat.
  2. While heating, sprinkle flesh side of salmon with salt.
  3. Press salted flesh into Blackened Seasoning. It'll be heavily coated.
  4. When oil's heated, remove from heat.
  5. One at a time, press the salmon fillets flesh side down into the oil.
  6. When done placing all filets in, put skillet back onto medium heat.
  7. Cook for 2-3 minutes, or until the salmon is blackened BUT not burnt.
  8. Flip filets onto skin side, cook for additional 4-6 minutes or 'till crispy.
  9. Transfer to plates and sprinkle with lemon zest and juice.
