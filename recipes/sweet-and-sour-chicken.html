Crock Pot Sweet and Sour Chicken

The meal that cooks itself!

Chinese food has been a staple of takeout dining, but
with it comes exorborant prices and an unhealthy meal.

This recipe will provide a very affordable, nutritious, and
insanely delicious meal with no need for any hardcore cooking.
A 4 ingredient meal which only requires prep and time.

Ingredients

  • At minimum, a 6 Qt. Crock-Pot or slow Cooker
  • 4 Boneless Skinless Chicken Breasts, Cubed
  • 1 Cup Orange Marmalade
  • 1/2 cup sweet chili sauce
  • 1/2 cup crushed pineapple
  • Salt & Pepper To Taste
  • White Rice for Serving With

Steps

  1. Spray Crock-Pot with Nonstick Spray.
  2. Place Chicken in the bottom, add salt/pepper if desired.
  3. Add Marmalade, Sweet Chili Sauce, and Crushed Pineapple.
  4. Toss to Combine All Ingredients.
  5. Cook on low for 4-5 hours, or until chicken's 165F.
  6. Serve over White Rice if desired!
