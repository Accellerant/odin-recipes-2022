Turkey Sloppy Joes

The simplest meal to make: Turkey Sloppy Joes!

Have you ever wanted a quick and easy meal which both kids and adults will love?

This simple recipe for Turkey Sloppy Joes will quickly become a quick favorite of all!

Ingredients

  • 1 lb. Ground Turkey
  • 1/2 Small Onion - Chopped
  • 1 Cup Ketchup
  • 1 Teaspoon Yellow Mustard
  • 1 Teaspoon Worcestershire Sauce
  • Salt & Pepper To Taste

Steps

  1. In medium skillet, brown turkey and onions at medium heat.
  2. Add Ketchup, Mustard, Worcestershire Sauce, and Salt/Pepper.
  3. Turn heat to medium-low, simmer for 10 minutes.
  4. Serve on your favorite bun!
